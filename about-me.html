Witcher 3: Signs

  • Aard

    Aard is a simple magical sign used by witchers. It is comprised of a telekinetic thrust that can stun, repel, knock down, or disarm opponents, as well as remove barriers and other objects.

  • Axii

    Axii is a simple magical sign comprised of a hypnotic effect and is used by witchers. It can calm down people and creatures, manipulate their minds or be used to hex enemies, causing them to fight alongside the caster.

  • Igni

    Igni is a simple magical sign used by witchers. It is comprised of a pyrokinetic burst that can repel and ignite opponents, as well as start fires.

  • Quen

    Quen is a simple magical sign used by witchers. When cast it forms a protective field around the casting witcher which protects him from sonic waves and similar attacks, but not from the physical blows.

  • Yrden

    Yrden is a simple magical sign used by witchers. When inscribed on a solid surface, it blocks the monsters from getting closer, scaring them off. If a hostile being enters the area affected by Sign, it is visibly slowed down, and non-corporeal forces become corporeal.
